Jack

I'm making this card of my best friend, Jack, but he would likely prefer that I instead take him for a walk around the neighborhood

  • Alias: Mr. Keen
  • Daily Activities: Lounging around, begging for food, barking incessantly at the mailman and any stray cats or wild squirrels who dare to enter our yard
  • Loves: walks outside, hanging out in the kitchen, getting gently scratched on the head, any offering of chicken
  • Hates: sudden movements, any loud, unexpected noises, cats sitting on stairs waiting for the perfect moment to strike

Jack weighs no more than ten pounds and his true size as a small dog is concealed by his long, soft toasted marshmallow colored fur. Since he was rescued from a shelter at a young age, the story behind his missing right-eye remains forever a mystery. This doesn't create any obstacle whatsoever from him being the cutest dog ever!
